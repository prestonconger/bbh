The Basics of Biblical Hebrew
Chapter 1
Overview
Learning to read biblical Hebrew begins with learning to read, recite, and write the Hebrew alphabet. This chapter teaches you the letters and sounds of the Hebrew alphabet, explaining key points to remember to identify them.

Watch the video to learn more about what you can expect to learn in this chapter.
Outcome and Objectives
- OUTCOME: As a result of this chapter, you will be able to read, write, and recite the Hebrew alphabet.
- OBJECTIVES: To successfully accomplish the above chapter outcome:
  • You will be able to identify the letters of the Hebrew alphabet in their various forms.
  • You will be able to articulate the sound(s) produced by each letter of the Hebrew alphabet.
  • You will be able to write the letters of the Hebrew alphabet in their various forms.
